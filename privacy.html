Privacy Policy for PageShotGo

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

PageShotGo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All screenshot captures and processing are performed entirely locally on your device.

2. How We Use Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function correctly. Here is how they are used:

  • activeTab & scripting: Used solely to capture the content and scroll the web page you are currently viewing.
  • storage & unlimitedStorage: Used temporarily to process the image data locally on your machine.
  • downloads: Used to save the final screenshot directly to your device.

3. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, trackers, or external servers. Your data never leaves your computer.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
